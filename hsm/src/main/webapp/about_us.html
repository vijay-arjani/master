C-tech Nutriscience Pvt. Ltd.

Established in the year 2009, at Pune (Maharashtra, India), we “C-Tech Nutriscience Pvt. Ltd” are a Private Limited Company and the foremost manufacturer and supplier of a wide array of Multipurpose Organic Fertilizer, Silver Nanoparticles, Water Absorber Polymer, Chelated Micronutrient Mixture, Liquid Sulphur, Fulvic Acid Powder, Gibberellic Acid Powder, Sea weed Powder, Humic Acid, etc. Our company is managed under the supervision of our mentor “Mr. Jagtap C.S. (Director)”. He possesses vast understanding and holds rich expertise. We offer our products under the brand name of C-Tech Tantra, Arrow-6, C-Sulf, etc.

Our Infrastructure

Owing to our well established and pioneering infrastructural unit, we have become competent enough to offer the best quality products to the customers. Our huge infrastructural facility is constructed over a large area of land and is further alienated into various units like production unit, quality testing unit, warehousing and logistics unit for streamlined business operations. All these segregated units are handled by our veteran team of professionals.

Why Us?

Since our establishment, our utmost aim is to render the maximum client satisfaction. We are engaged in offering optimum quality chemicals to the customers.
